Football4u

This event we organize during the Conditional Control Movement Order (CMCO) in Malaysia. The purpose of this event is to give the students in IIUM get released their tension of studying and strengthen friendship between each other. This event will be handled with full Standard Operation Procedure (SOP) that was designed by the Malaysia government. Thus, we make our special football rules for this event such as we limit the players for each team for 11 players to 7 players. There is prizes and medals for each teams and many more. All the informations are represented here.

RULES

This Tournament will be held under the rules of CMCO. So, there are specific rules and regulations. We also have some regulations for Player Registration and Eligibility rules. Further details are given in Rules page.
